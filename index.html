Next event: StudioCo.de = Community

We're having an event, and we'd love for you to come. Greg Ash, Creative Director of 160over90 (Gainesville) will be speaking on our theme: Community. It's what we're celebrating, after all. We love the idea of our creative community getting together and sharing some inspiration and goodness.

About StudioCo.de

StudioCo.de is a local organization whose mission is to promote creativity, networking and collaboration. We're new in town, but we've got a lot of awesome planned. We'll be hosting a monthly lecture series, providing educational opportunities, and putting together workshops for the Gainesville creative community.

Just so we're on the same page, we use the term creative pretty broadly — artists, designers, students, developers, artisans, photographers, calligraphers, knitters, illustrators, sculptors, creative directors, welders, bakers, stylists and anyone else who considers themselves to be creative — we want you all to feel included.

We hope you'll join us!
